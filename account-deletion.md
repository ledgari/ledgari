# Ledgari Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their Ledgari account and associated data.

To request account deletion, please send an email to:

**ledgariapp@gmail.com**

Use the subject:
**Account Deletion Request**

Please include the following details in your message:

- Account email address  
- Business name

You can also click [here](mailto:ledgariapp@gmail.com?subject=Account%20Deletion%20Request&body=I%20want%20to%20request%20deletion%20of%20my%20account%20on%20Ledgari%20with%20the%20following%20details:%0A%0AEmail:%20%0ABusiness%20Name:%20
) to take you directly to your mail app with a message template

Deletion requests are processed within **30 days**.
